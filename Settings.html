Settings

Export Encryption

Check Enable encryption box to encrypt export data archives and protect them with the provided password. Change password by pressing the circular error button. After encryption, only people who know the password could open the data archives.

Global note

Specify text in the Global Note field to be shown on the top of every screen on the server.

Company logo

Company logo shows a custom image on the server login page and on the login page of the interviewer app. Click Choose file button to upload an image on your computer to the server.
